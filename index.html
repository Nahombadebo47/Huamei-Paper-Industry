OUR MISSION

At Huamei Paper Industries, our mission is to provide premium quality paper and soft tissue products that combine manufacturing expertise with the vibrant opportunities in Ethiopia. We value integrity, quality, and community. Integrity guides our business practices and relationships, ensuring transparency and trust. Quality is at the heart of our products, reflecting our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Community drives us to support local initiatives, create jobs, and build a better future together with the people of Ethiopia.
